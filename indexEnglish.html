MISSION

"ALLPA" is an organization dedicated to the marketing of artisanal products, both nationally and internationally. Its products originate from Santiago del Estero, Argentina. They are made with 100% organic materials and crafted in an artisanal way by communities of indigenous peoples in northern Argentina. Our team seeks to establish a connection with our producers, achieving a social impact and economic independence. This connection ensures the highest quality and efficiency in our products, aiming to bring the northern Argentinean essence to the world."

TALENT STORIES

In the northern region of Argentina, a group of skilled weavers, mostly comprised of women, has cultivated an ancestral talent in textile creation. Across generations, they have woven stories of the region with patience and dedication, passed down from grandmothers to granddaughters. Their expert hands have transformed simple threads into masterpieces that reflect everyday life and the rich local cultural heritage. These artisans have adapted their techniques to modernity, incorporating contemporary designs without losing the essence oftheir tradition. Whether vibrant shawls or meticulously crafted blankets, their textiles represent the evolution of an artdeeply rooted in history. In each of their weavings, they proudly share their creations, preserving their legacy and maintaining the connection betweenpast and present. Every purchase of their textiles contributes to keeping this tradition alive, supporting these women andensuring the transmission of their skills to future generations. Thus, the talent of these skilled weavers stands as a living testament to the importance of preserving cultural roots thatshape the identity of the region.
